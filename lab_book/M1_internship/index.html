Intestine staining protocol:

Day 1

  1. Dissect guts in PBS at RT°C (or in ice if staining for e.g. P-H3).
  2. Fix 2h in 8% PFA at RT°C, no shaking. prepare BSA during that time
  3. Coat a pipette tip in PBT by pipetting up and down. Use this emptied tip to take off the PFA. (the guts might stick to the tip if not coated)
  4. Rinse 1× with PBT
  5. Block 30 min in 1% BSA in PBT, shaking.
  6. Add Primary antibodies in 1% BSA in PBT overnight at +4°C, shaking.

Day 2

  1. Rinse 2× + wash in PBT, shaking, while preparing the 2nd antibody.
  2. Add secondary antibodies in 1% BSA in PBT for 1h (30 min if in a rush) at RT°C, shaking.
  3. Rinse 2× and leave washing for 3-4h at RT°C in PBT.
  4. Remove the PBT and add Vectashield + DAPI mounting media 30 µL.

Few days prior to imaging

  1. Mount on slides with slightly indented circles to avoid coverslip crushing

Example primary antibodies

  • Anti-prospero (mouse, nuclear) 1:1000
  • Anti-β-gal (rabbit, labelled with “MP”) 1:400
  • Anti-β-gal (mouse, labelled with “40-1a-s”, Box 1 B9) 1:20
  • Anti-PWP1 (rabbit, labelled with “21351 #4”, Box 6 I2 at -20°C) 1:500
  • Anti-Delta (mouse, membrane bound) 1:1000

Dilute secondary antibodies 1:1000

With samples that don’t need antibodies, skip steps 4-8.

Squishing & PCR/gel

Single males for the step C of the cross were taken for the squishing process:

Single fly extraction:

Squishing buffer prepared on ice, 50 µL in milliQ water per flie:

  • Tris HCl pH 8.0 -> 10 mM
  • NaCl -> 25 mM
  • EDTA -> 1 mM
  • Proteinase K -> 200 µg.mL-1 Add to the mix at the very end, after transferring the males in the tubes

Which gives for our stocks:

  • Tris HCl pH 8.0 [3 M] = 0.166 µL
  • NaCl [5 M] = 0.25 µL
  • EDTA [0.5 M] = 0.1 µL
  • Proteinase K = 0.5 µL
  • H2O = 48.984 µL

Males transfered in a PCR tubes on ice, 50 µL of squishing buffer per tube.
“It’s squishing time”, and proceed to squish all over the place.

Then DNA extraction:

  • 30 minutes at 37°C
  • 10 minutes at 85°C

This result can be freezed at -20°C.

Then the PCR/gel was done.

PCR/gel:

Master mix for 25 µL total = 22 µL mix + 3 µL flie stew

  • 10× buffer -> 2.5 µL
  • 10 mM dNTPs -> 0.5 µL
  • F primer 10 µM -> 0.5 µL
  • R primer 10 µM -> 0.5 µL
  • Taq DNA pol -> 0.125 µL
  • H₂O molecular grade -> 17.875 µL

With the addition of:

  • Template DNA -> 3 µL

Temperatures:

  • 95°C – 30 sec
  • repeat 30×
    • 95°C – 20 sec
    • 60°C – 30 sec
    • 68°C – 45 sec
  • 68°C – 5 min
  • 10°C – end

1% agarose gel:

  • 150 mL of 1× TAE
  • 1.5 g of agarose

Ladder preparation:

  • 2 µL ladder (100 bp or 1kb)
  • 1 µL Midori Green Direct
  • 7 µL H₂O molecular grade

In each sample add 2.5 µL Midori Green Direct (1:10).
